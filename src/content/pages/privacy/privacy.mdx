##Privacy Policy

**1. Introduction**

Welcome to Ethereum Classic Cooperative, Inc. (“we”, “us”, or “our”). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it through our compliance with this policy.

This policy describes the types of information we may collect from you or that you may provide when you visit our website etccooperative.org (our "Website") and our practices for collecting, using, maintaining, protecting, and disclosing that information.

**2. Information We Collect About You**

Our website itself does not collect any personal information that can be used to identify you, such as your name, postal address, e-mail address, or telephone number.

However, we do collect information about your internet connection, the equipment you use to access our website, and usage details. This information is about you but does not identify you.

Please be aware that our website may contain links to other sites, and these other sites may collect personal information about you. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these other sites and we encourage you to review the privacy policies of each site you visit.

**3. How We Use Your Information**

We do not use personal information to present our website and its contents to you. However, we do use information about your internet connection and the equipment you use to access our website for analytical purposes, such as understanding user behavior and trends to improve our website.

We may also use this information to:

- Provide you with information or services that you request from us;
- Fulfill any other purpose for which you provide it;
- Notify you about changes to our website or any services we offer or provide through it.

**4. Disclosure of Your Information**

We may disclose aggregated information about our users, and information that does not identify any individual, without restriction. We may disclose personal information that we collect or you provide as described in this privacy policy.

**5. Changes to Our Privacy Policy**

It is our policy to post any changes we make to our privacy policy on this page. If we make material changes to how we treat our users' personal information, we will notify you by email to the email address specified in your account or through a notice on the website home page.
